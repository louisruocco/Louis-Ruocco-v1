About Me


Hi there! My name is Louis Ruocco. I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I am a 22 year old graduate based in the UK. Coding, particularly Web Development is a real passion of mine and I aim to one day become a professional Full Stack Developer. Currently, I work as an IT Engineer for Expert IT.

I have been coding for the last two years, in between completing a bachelor's degree. This was mainly down to some common misconceptions that I had regarding Coding. For example, needing to be a mathematical genius in order to code. I haven't looked back since I made my first "Hello World" application! I really enjoy building new websites and applications and it gives me a great sense of satisfaction to see a project through to the end.
